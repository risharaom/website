This is Code4Cause

Where teens empower youth to turn code, analytics, and advocacy into impact.

Overview

Code4Cause is a youth-led organization committed to empowering students to use technology for good. We bring together driven teens to tackle real-world social and environmental challenges through data science, machine learning, and coding.

Through hands-on projects, workshops, and mentorship, we equip young changemakers with the skills, tools, and community support to create meaningful impact. At Code4Cause, we’re not just learning to code — we’re coding to make a difference.

What We Do

Ethical tech

Developing applications and solutions to address problems in the Bay Area.

Data for Good

Using data analytics, data visualization, and machine learning to drive evidence-based action.

Community Advocacy

Empowering youth with technology skills to advocate for positive change in their communities.
