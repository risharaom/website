Meet the Team

We are a group of student changemakers using technology to make a difference.

Our team brings together coders, designers, data scientists, and leaders who believe in using tech for good. Whether it’s building models to save trees or organizing community events, we’re united by one goal: empowering youth through impactful innovation.

Core Team

Risha Rao

Role: CTO

As Chief Technology Officer at Code4Cause, I lead the development and strategy behind all technical initiatives. I manage our website, ensuring it remains a functional, engaging hub for our community. I guide students through hands-on coding sessions, helping them build real-world projects with purpose. I also design reusable code templates and architect machine learning models that students can explore, expand, and customize. My work ensures that every participant—regardless of experience—has a strong technical foundation and the tools to turn ideas into impactful, data-driven solutions. I collaborate closely with the team to keep our tech aligned with our mission of empowering youth through innovation.

Sanvi Bharadwaj

Role: CMO

As Chief Marketing Officer at Code4Cause, I lead the strategy behind our outreach team. I design campaigns that amplify our mission, engage students and families, and grow our platform across digital and community spaces. Beyond social media and visual branding, I also coordinate advocacy efforts, contacting local city councils and community leaders to present student research and push for data-driven change. I work closely with the team to align messaging with our values and bring visibility to the work our students and team do.

Aarya Patel

Role: COO

As Chief Operating Officer, I coordinate our program operations, develop organizational systems, and ensure the smooth execution of our 8-week sessions. I work closely with all teams to align our efforts and oversee program logistics from planning to implementation. I manage our timelines, resources, and help solve challenges that arise along the way.

What Drives Us

We come from different schools and cities across the Bay Area, but we all share the same passion: using our coding skills for community-driven solutions. Whether it's environmental protection, health advocacy, or accessibility, we're committed to change.
