Upcoming Projects

Check out our current and upcoming projects that aim to make a positive impact through technology. Click on each project to learn more.

Wildlife Tracker App

A mobile application to help track and monitor local wildlife populations.

Status: In Development Launch: June 2025

The Wildlife Tracker App will allow citizen scientists to document wildlife sightings in local ecosystems. Data collected will assist conservation efforts and environmental research.

Tech Stack: Flutter, Firebase, Machine Learning for species identification

Looking for team members with mobile development experience!

Community Resource Map

Interactive map showing free and low-cost resources for underserved communities.

Status: Planning Launch: August 2025

This web application will map and categorize community resources like food banks, healthcare services, and educational opportunities to help connect those in need with available support.

Tech Stack: JavaScript, React, Leaflet Maps API, MongoDB

Seeking collaborators with database and mapping experience.

Climate Data Dashboard

Visualizing local climate data to increase awareness and drive action.

Status: Recruiting Launch: September 2025

This interactive dashboard will visualize climate change data for our local region, making complex environmental data accessible to everyone, especially students and policymakers.

Tech Stack: Python, D3.js, React, Tableau

Looking for team members with data visualization and analysis skills!
